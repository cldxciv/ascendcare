Autism Services

AlphaBee, for 20+ years has been delivering clinically proven services for individuals with ASD and related exceptionalities. Through personalized, evidence-based therapies, AlphaBee supports individuals in achieving their full potential. By collaborating closely with caregivers, educational teams, and external professionals, AlphaBee provides comprehensive programming that addresses each child’s developmental journey.

Our Commitment

At AlphaBee, we are dedicated to being at the forefront of advancements in research and best practices for children with Autism. We continuously enhance the skills and knowledge of our team to provide cutting-edge, evidence-based practices. Our unwavering commitment ensures the children and families we serve receive the highest standard of learning and support to foster growth and meaningful progress.

Who We Serve?

AlphaBee is dedicated to supporting individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorder and other developmental disabilities, providing a learner-centered approach and dedicated support for children and teens aged between 12 months and 18 years.
