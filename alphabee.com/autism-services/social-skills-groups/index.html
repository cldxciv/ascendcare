What is AlphaBee's Social Skills Group?

AlphaBee offers Social Skills Groups designed to help children develop essential social skills through engaging, interactive experiences. These evidence-based groups provide support for children with different social skill levels, focusing on building meaningful peer interactions and communication abilities. Our program is designed by experienced RBA’s ensuring high-quality instruction and individualized attention.

Our Cooperative Activities Include:
  • Interactive games
  • Science experiments
  • Arts and crafts
  • Role-playing scenarios
  • Daily living skills

These activities are designed to make learning social skills enjoyable while providing opportunities for practical application. By combining fun, structured experiences with skill-building objectives, we create an environment where children can learn and grow naturally.

AlphaBee's Social Skills Group Programs

Program benefits:

Participation in our social skills groups may lead to:

  • Improved social confidence
  • Enhanced communication abilities
  • Better peer relationships
  • Increased emotional regulation
  • Developed problem-solving skills
  • Improved ability to form and maintain friendship

Our Commitment

At AlphaBee, we are dedicated to being at the forefront of advancements in research and best practices for children with Autism. We continuously enhance the skills and knowledge of our team to provide cutting-edge, evidence-based practices. Our unwavering commitment ensures the children and families we serve receive the highest standard of learning and support to foster growth and meaningful progress.

Where We Deliver Services?

AlphaBee delivers services across multiple locations, including Toronto, Peel, York, Halton and the Greater Hamilton area with AlphaBee centres located in Etobicoke, Scarborough, and Hamilton, as well as online, home-based, and, school/daycare settings.
